FreePad

We need your help!

Thousands of young girls don't have access to menstruation pad. This app is designed to encourage people, especially women to provide free pad to young women in their neighborhood. A young girl in Osogbo will indicate interest and a donor can ask her to pick it up at nearest pharmacy or store at no cost
